Development team of TourTrak

The official mobile application of the TD Five Boro Bike Tour

A Better Bike Tour

Bike New York, the non-profit organization who hosts the TD Five Boro Bike Tour, uses TourTrak to better manage the tour. Each year, around 32,000 riders participate in the race. With TourTrak, Bike New York can track the speed and location of each rider and identify and handle bottlenecks.

Rider-centric Features

Using location data, TourTrak provides users with an interactive map of the TD Five Boro Bike Tour that shows useful points of interest nearby them as they progress along the route. TourTrak also contains an extensive FAQ about the TD Five Boro Bike Tour to provide riders with all the information they need.

Cross-Platform

TourTrak is designed to support both iOS and Android devices, in order to best suit the needs of the TD Five Boro Bike Tour's riders.

An Open Source Solution

This year's TD Five Boro Bike Tour is only the beginning for TourTrak. The goal of our project is for TourTrak to exist beyond this event and beyond our senior year as an open source solution for future Bike New York events and events hosted by similar organizations. All our code is hosted in Github repositories, and available to the public. Want to help continue the work for next year's tour, or build your own version of TourTrak to suit your needs?
